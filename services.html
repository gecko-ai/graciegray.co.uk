Services & Support

Bespoke, in home and virtual support for mothers and families. In person across Bath, Somerset and the southwest. Virtual support nationally and internationally

Core Postnatal Support

Child-led support

When you bring a new baby home, finding meaningful time for your older children can feel impossible. The guilt is real, and it's heavy.

With child-led support, I conduct engaging, educational, and interactive activities with your older children, keeping them stimulated, secure, and happy, while you enjoy completely guilt-free, uninterrupted bonding time with your newborn.

Your older child feels seen, loved, and valued. Your baby gets the quiet, focused attention they need. You get to breathe.

"Helping your child step into their new role, not just adjust, but truly thrive."
- Grace Gray
Core Postnatal Support

Baby-led support

Sometimes, what you need most is the freedom to be fully present with your older child. To sit on the floor and build the tower. To read the story without one ear on the monitor. To reassure them, with your presence and your time, that they still matter just as much as they always did.

With baby-led support, I expertly hold, soothe, and care for your newborn, giving you the physical and emotional space to give dedicated, focused one-on-one time to your older children.

Specialised support

Beyond the early days

From preparing for birth through to navigating the complex emotions of trauma recovery, I offer a comprehensive range of support tailored to your family's specific needs, rooted in the understanding that postpartum has no time limit.

Sibling Transition

Leveraging my Diploma in Child Behaviour, I help older children feel settled, safe, and secure as they step into their new role as a big brother or sister. This is about more than adjustment; it's about helping them thrive.

Birth Trauma Support

For mothers whose birth did not go to plan. I provide deeply empathetic emotional, practical, and physical postnatal support, backed by specialist training and the lived experience of navigating the unexpected myself.

Antenatal Preparation

Supporting mothers as they prepare for birth - whether vaginal or caesarean - helping them feel informed, ready, and emotionally held before the baby arrives. This is for all births. Whether this is your first or your fourth, the right preparation makes all the difference.

C-Section Support & Preparation

An abdominal birth is still a birth. Just because you are having a caesarean does not mean your birth is out of your control. C-section preparation is a vital part of the process - understanding what to expect, how to advocate for yourself, and how to create a calm, positive environment can transform your experience. I help mothers prepare physically and emotionally so that they feel informed, empowered, and held throughout every stage of their birth.

Virtual Support

National and international online support for mothers navigating matrescence, postnatal recovery, or simply needing expert guidance from someone who truly understands. Available wherever you are.

Every family is entirely different.

Whether you need short-term postnatal recovery support or ongoing family integration, I will build a package that fits your exact needs and circumstances.
